Notes:
	*Changing ATP and other stats will revert to their original values upon
leveling up or re-equipping an item.
	*In the spell list 00 means the end of that characters spell list.  If you
fill the Hero’s spell list completely, remove the 00 in front of Lufia’s list,
the Hero’s list will then spill over into Lufia’s.
       During battle if you want to have the original characters and not the
regular game characters, Maxim, Guy, Selan, and Artea, set the 20ED hex value
to 01, 00 equals normal characters.
	Each characters stats are side by side for ATP, Weapon equipped, and so on. 
Just in case if you did not notice and want to set all stats to max or the same.
	The character list are the characters that you have in their respective order.
