                            STATISTIC GROWTH CHARTS
###############################################################################

The following charts depict the base amount by which statistics are increased
at level up. If the value is greater than zero, there is a fifty-fifty chance
that it will be increased by one, and a one-in-four chance that it will be
decreased by the same amount instead.

Note that because of a bug in the code, whenever the algorithm reaches either
the 257th or 513th table entry during a level up, it rolls back by 256 entries.
The relevant entries in the charts are enclosed in parentheses to denote this
fact and alleviate potential confusion.
