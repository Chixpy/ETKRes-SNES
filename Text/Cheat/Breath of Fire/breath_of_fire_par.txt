-----------
Note:
*Move the character you want to levelup to position 1,2 or 3

7e1090 00  =No random battles
7e0fb1 ff  =65535 exp gain in battle.
7e5021 ff  =Max Money
7e1056 ff  =Character position 1 level 99 1 battle
7e10f6 ff  =Character position 2 level 99 1 battle
7e1196 ff  -Character position 3 level 99 1 battle

Note:
*Item                             Type is 01
*Wepons                           Type is 11
*Armor,Shields,Helmets,Rings,etc  type is 21

*The number 1 in the end of the type code is the quantity of the items.
*In Weapons,Armors,Shields,Helmets,Rings,etc leave the number 1.
*You can change the quantity of the items from 1 to 9

----------------------------------
Item Codes
7e5140 XX =Item Digit
7e5141 XX =Item type,Item Quantity
----------------------------------

Example:
For the DragonHt put the code:

        7e5140 8a=DragonHT

Now put the code for the Item Type in this case a Helmet 21

        7e5141 21=Armor,Shields,Helmets,Rings,etc
