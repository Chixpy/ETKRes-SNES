###############################################################################
                                 GENERAL NOTES
###############################################################################

Legend for locations:
[A] The item is obtained by means of fishing or hunting.
[C] The listed characters are initially equipped with this item.
[D] The item can be found by digging the ground with Mogu in a specific region.
[E] The item is obtained through an event.
[G] The item lies on the ground. The direction faced is normally irrelevant.
[M] The listed monsters may drop this item.
[P] The item is obtained on the overworld map by punching a tree with Ox.
[S] The item is available in the listed shops.
[T] The item lies inside a treasure chest or within a dresser.

Miscellaneous notes:
1. One of the elements has yet to be named and is currently listed as [???].
2. Sr-1, Sr-2, and Sr-3 can equip any item that is compatible with Gobi.
3. Monsters that cannot normally be defeated are not listed.
4. The value of items that cannot be sold is always zero; this is not listed.
5. Money amounts for treasure chests have been removed, but may be reinserted.
6. Elemental resistances are established by processing equipment in the
   following order: shield, armor, helmet. Accessories are thus disregarded.
   Once a piece of equipment with elemental properties is detected, the
   procedure terminates, thereby neglecting the rest of the equipment.
